This Authorization Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between:

PhishGuard India ("Service Provider")
and

EFFECTIVE DATE: _______________


1. PURPOSE

The Client authorizes PhishGuard India to conduct simulated phishing email and
messaging campaigns ("Simulations") targeting the Client's own employees, for
the sole purpose of cybersecurity awareness training and risk assessment.

2. SCOPE OF AUTHORIZATION

2.1. The Client confirms it has the legal right and internal authority to
     authorize security testing of the email addresses and contact details
     it provides to PhishGuard India.

2.2. Simulations will only be sent to email addresses and phone numbers
     explicitly provided by the Client for this purpose.

2.3. PhishGuard India will never spoof or impersonate the actual, real
     domain of any government body, bank, or third party. All simulation
     content uses dedicated sending infrastructure clearly distinct from
     the entities referenced in template content (e.g. "HDFC Bank",
     "GST Department") for illustrative/training purposes only.

2.4. Every simulated phishing page and post-click training page will carry
     a visible disclosure identifying it as a PhishGuard India simulation.

3. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1. The Client will inform relevant internal stakeholders (e.g. IT/Security
     leadership, HR) that simulated phishing campaigns may occur during the
     term of this Agreement, without revealing specific timing to the
     employees being tested.

3.2. The Client is responsible for ensuring that providing employee contact
     information to PhishGuard India complies with the Client's own internal
     data protection policies and any applicable employment agreements.

3.3. The Client will not use PhishGuard India's services to target,
     harass, or embarrass any specific individual employee maliciously.

4. DATA HANDLING

4.1. PhishGuard India will store employee names, email addresses, and
     campaign interaction data (click events, timestamps, device/browser
     metadata) solely for the purpose of delivering this service.

4.2. Data will be hosted on infrastructure located in India (Mumbai region)
     and will not be sold, shared, or used for any purpose beyond the
     Client's awareness training program.

4.3. Upon termination of this Agreement, the Client may request deletion
     of all employee data within 30 days, subject to any reports already
     generated for the Client's own compliance records.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

5.1. PhishGuard India is not liable for any disruption, embarrassment, or
     workplace incident arising from an employee's response to a simulation,
     provided PhishGuard India has acted in accordance with this Agreement.

5.2. PhishGuard India's total liability under this Agreement is limited to
     the fees paid by the Client in the preceding 3 months.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION

6.1. This Agreement remains in effect for the duration of the Client's
     active subscription and may be terminated by either party with 7
     days' written notice.

6.2. No new Simulations will be launched after termination notice is given,
     though previously scheduled campaigns may complete.

7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be
subject to the jurisdiction of the courts in [Your City], Rajasthan.
