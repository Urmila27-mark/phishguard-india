Questions

Is this legal? Aren't you basically phishing people?+
Every campaign requires a signed authorization from the business before it launches — the same legal framework used by every authorized security testing firm globally, under IT Act 2000 provisions for authorized testing. We never spoof real domains, and every simulation page carries a visible disclosure tag proving it's a controlled test.
Will this get flagged as spam by Gmail or Outlook?+
We use dedicated, properly configured sending domains with SPF/DKIM authentication — the same setup real businesses use. This ensures deliverability while keeping the simulation realistic.

Most businesses are live with their first campaign in under 10 minutes. Upload a CSV of employees, pick a template, hit launch. No integration or IT involvement required.

Yes — all training content is available in Hindi and English, with more Indian languages on the roadmap based on customer demand.

Employee data is encrypted, hosted on infrastructure in the Mumbai AWS region, and never shared with third parties. You can request full deletion at any time.
